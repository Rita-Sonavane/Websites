Motto & Purpose

Motto

UNEXO, an innovative platform under the umbrella of OBOCOW, is dedicated to transforming lives and communities by seamlessly merging the digital and physical realms. With a 'phygital' approach, it combines the convenience of the digital world with the warmth of physical presence, fostering a sense of community in cities and rural towns alike. UNEXO's primary focus areas include job creation, supporting the gig economy, providing essential healthcare and education services, and enhancing food & beverage experiences. It operates as a venture philanthropy, sharing profits to uplift the vulnerable and disadvantaged, ultimately striving to empower individuals to join the middle class while promoting global member enjoyment and networking

Purpose

UNEXO's purpose is to bridge the divide between digital convenience and physical community, enriching lives and nurturing inclusivity. We are committed to creating opportunities, fostering a sense of belonging, and improving the well-being of individuals and communities worldwide. Through our 'phygital' approach, we aim to empower, educate, and uplift, with a special focus on supporting those in need. UNEXO operates as a catalyst for positive change, sharing our successes to drive social advancement and elevate the most vulnerable among us into the middle class, all while enhancing global member enjoyment and connectivity.
