About Us

The Best Solution With Experience

ODENOK TOBACCO CRESCENT LTD (OTC LTD) is an ambitious tobacco company specializing in the distribution of high-quality tobacco products within the Indian market. With a team boasting a combined expertise of 1,200 years in the tobacco industry, we are determined to establish ourselves as a market leader in India. In the dynamic and competitive landscape of the Indian tobacco market, OTC LTD stands out as a company driven by a deep understanding of local preferences and market dynamics. Our team's extensive experience and knowledge allow us to navigate the intricacies of the Indian tobacco industry with agility and precision. While OTC LTD is relatively new, our team's collective expertise empowers us to make strategic decisions, identify emerging trends, and deliver products that resonate with Indian consumers. We are committed to exceeding customer expectations by offering a diverse range of high-quality tobacco blends that cater to the discerning tastes of Indian tobacco enthusiasts. As we strive to become a market leader in India, we prioritize responsible practices, sustainability, and ethical conduct.
